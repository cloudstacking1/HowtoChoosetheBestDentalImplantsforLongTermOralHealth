What Are Dental Implants?

Dental implants are titanium posts surgically inserted into your jawbone to serve as artificial roots for replacement teeth. They provide a sturdy foundation for both fixed and removable prosthetic teeth. Whether you need a single tooth implant, all-on-4 dental implants, or full arch restoration, implants are a reliable solution.

Key Features

  • Types of Dental Implants

  • Single Tooth Implant

    • Perfect for those missing just one tooth. A single titanium post supports a crown that blends seamlessly with your existing teeth.

  • Multiple Teeth Implants

    • If you’re missing several teeth, implants can anchor bridges or individual crowns to fill the gap securely.

  • All-on-4 Dental Implants

    • This innovative technique allows a full arch of teeth to be supported on just four implants. It’s ideal for those who have lost all or most of their teeth.

  • Same Day Dental Implants

    • Also known as immediate load implants, these allow the placement of temporary teeth on the same day as the implant procedure, minimizing downtime.

The Dental Implant Procedure Step-by-Step

  • Initial Consultation: Includes oral exam, digital X-rays, and treatment plan.

  • Implant Placement: The titanium post is surgically inserted into the jawbone.

  • Healing Period: Osseointegration occurs as the implant fuses with the bone.

  • Abutment Placement: A small connector is placed on the implant post.

  • Crown Attachment: A custom-made crown is secured to the abutment.

    • Who is a Candidate for Dental Implants?

    You may be a good candidate if you:
